About Foodie Express

Foodie Express is a food discovery website that allows users to explore different types of delicious dishes. Our platform focuses on providing a clean and simple interface for users to browse meals easily.

The project was developed as part of a learning initiative to understand frontend development and user interface design.

Our Mission

Our mission is to create an easy and enjoyable food browsing experience for users. We aim to showcase tasty meals and promote food discovery through a modern website interface.

Foodie Express focuses on simplicity, attractive design, and smooth navigation to make the user experience better.

Fresh & Delicious Food

We believe that good food brings people together. Our website displays different varieties of dishes prepared with fresh ingredients and rich flavors to inspire food lovers.

Through this project we aim to demonstrate how a food ordering interface can be designed using HTML and CSS.
